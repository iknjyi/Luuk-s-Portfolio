Drop your Trailers / Teasers MP4 files in this folder.

Expected filenames (referenced in lib/data.ts):
  trailer-01.mp4   (16:9 wide clip)
  teaser-01.mp4    (16:9 wide clip)

You can use different filenames — just update the matching `videoSrc`
path on each project in lib/data.ts to point at your file instead.

Delete this text file once your real videos are in place (it has no
effect on the site either way).
